About Me

Telling Stories That Matter

I'm a broadcast and digital journalist graduating from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University in May 2026, with a minor in Political Science. At Newhouse, I report across platforms including NCC News, one of the premier student-run TV and radio stations in the country, and The Daily Orange, the nation's oldest independent student newspaper.

My experience spans from producing and anchoring live newscasts to interning with Bruce Beck of NBC Sports New York, where I served as Assistant Director of his broadcasting camp at Iona University. I've also worked with Foul Territory on daily live podcast production and was selected for the Maccabi USA Media Team.

Sports remain my dream beat, but I'm driven by curiosity and a commitment to impactful storytelling wherever journalism takes me.
